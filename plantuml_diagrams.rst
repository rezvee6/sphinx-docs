Complex PlantUML Diagram with Pan/Zoom
======================================

Below is a **complex example** showing a mixture of components and a
sequence diagram for a fictional microservices-based system. The
diagram is rendered via PlantUML into SVG, and a small bit of
JavaScript enables pan/zoom in the HTML output.

.. note::

   - Use **mouse wheel / trackpad** to zoom in and out.
   - **Click and drag** to pan the diagram.
   - Double-click to reset the view.
